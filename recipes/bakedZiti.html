Carmela's Baked Ziti

Description

Carmela Soprano's Baked Ziti recipe is a classic Italian-American dish that is both comforting and delicious. The key ingredients for this recipe include ziti pasta, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, marinara sauce, eggs, and ground beef or sausage (optional).

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound (450g) ziti pasta
  2. 2 cups (475g) ricotta cheese
  3. 2 large eggs, beaten
  4. 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 3 cups (340g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  6. 4 cups (about 960g) marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  7. 1 pound (450g) ground beef or sausage (optional, for meat sauce)
  8. Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Note: The measurements provided are approximate and can be adjusted based on your preference or the number of servings you wish to make. Additionally, feel free to customize the recipe by adding other spices or ingredients to suit your taste. Enjoy your delicious Baked Ziti!
