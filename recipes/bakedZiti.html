Carmela's Baked Ziti

Description

Carmela Soprano's Baked Ziti recipe is a classic Italian-American dish that is both comforting and delicious.

The key ingredients for this recipe include ziti pasta, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, marinara sauce, eggs, and ground beef or sausage (optional).

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ziti pasta
  • 2 cups (475g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups (340g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 cups (about 960g) marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or sausage (optional, for meat sauce)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Note: The measurements provided are approximate and can be adjusted based on your preference or the number of servings you wish to make. Additionally, feel free to customize the recipe by adding other spices or ingredients to suit your taste. Enjoy your delicious Baked Ziti!

Steps

  1. Cook the ziti pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, beaten eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and a portion of the shredded mozzarella cheese. This mixture serves as the filling.
  3. Brown ground beef or sausage in a skillet (optional). Once cooked, drain any excess grease and add marinara sauce to the meat. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to a specified temperature (usually around 375°F or 190°C).
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce at the bottom.
  6. Layer half of the cooked ziti over the sauce.
  7. Spread the ricotta cheese mixture evenly on top of the ziti layer.
  8. Add another layer of ziti on top of the ricotta mixture.
  9. Pour the remaining meat sauce over the second ziti layer, ensuring it's evenly distributed.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the meat sauce.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Let the baked ziti cool slightly before serving, and garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
