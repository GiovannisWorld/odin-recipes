Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe


Description

Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe is a delightful and wholesome Italian pasta dish that brings together the earthy flavors of broccoli rabe, the satisfying texture of orecchiette pasta, and the delightful combination of garlic, chili flakes, and Parmesan cheese. This rustic and comforting recipe captures the essence of traditional Italian cuisine and is sure to become a favorite on your dining table.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces (340g) orecchiette pasta
  • 1 bunch of fresh broccoli rabe (rapini)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable broth or white wine
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Sun-dried tomatoes (optional, for added flavor)
  • Toasted pine nuts (optional, for added crunch)
  • Fresh lemon juice (optional, for a tangy twist)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the orecchiette pasta and cook it according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the broccoli rabe by trimming the tough ends and any large leaves. Blanch the broccoli rabe in a separate pot of boiling salted water for about 2-3 minutes until it becomes tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the extra-virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté until the garlic becomes golden and fragrant.
  4. Add the blanched broccoli rabe to the skillet and sauté it with the garlic oil for a few minutes until the flavors meld together.
  5. Pour in the vegetable broth or white wine, and let the mixture simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the broccoli rabe is tender-crisp.
  6. Add the cooked orecchiette pasta to the skillet with the broccoli rabe mixture. Toss everything together, ensuring the pasta is well coated with the flavors.
  7. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and season the dish with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Toss again to combine.
  8. If desired, add optional ingredients such as sun-dried tomatoes, toasted pine nuts, or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to enhance the dish's flavor and texture.
  9. Serve the Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe hot, and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
  10. Enjoy your comforting and delicious Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe, savoring the heartwarming taste of Italian flavors!
